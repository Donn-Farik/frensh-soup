French Onion

French Onion Soup We have been trying French onion soup in restaurants
for years and my family and friends agree none can compare to my recipe
for taste and simplicity of preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cups sliced onions
  • 4 (10.5 ounce) cans beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons dry sherry (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme salt and
    pepper to taste
  • 4 slices French bread 4 slices
    provolone cheese
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese
  • diced 1/4 cup
    grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Melt butter with olive oil in an 8 quart stock pot on medium heat.
    Add onions and continually stir until tender and translucent.
    Do not brown the onions
  2. Add beef broth, sherry and thyme. Season with salt and pepper,
    and simmer for 30 minutes
  3. Heat the oven broiler
  4. Ladle soup into oven safe serving bowls and place one slice of bread
    on top of each (bread may be broken into pieces if you prefer).
    Layer each slice of bread with a slice of provolone, 1/2 slice diced
    Swiss and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese. Place bowls on cookie sheet
    and broil in the preheated oven until cheese bubbles and browns slightly.
